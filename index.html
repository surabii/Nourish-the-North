Our Work

5.8 million Canadians live in food-insecure households. We’re on
a mission to change that.

Food insecurity is more than hunger; it's the struggle for access to nutritious food
due to financial constraints. Our mission is to eradicate this stress, creating a future
where no one wonders about their next meal.
